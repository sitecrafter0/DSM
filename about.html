About DSM Home Improvement

DSM Home Improvement was founded in 1995 with a clear purpose — to bring trust, professionalism, and integrity to South Africa’s construction and project management industries.

Our Vision

To pioneer economic empowerment in Civil & Building Construction, Renovations, Building & Environmental Maintenance, Road Maintenance, Cleaning & Sanitation Services, Project Management, Supply & Distribution, Training Industry, IT Solutions, Fleet Monitoring Solutions, Security Solutions, Transport Industry, and Agriculture across South Africa.

Our Mission

To be the preferred supplier of choice by providing superior value, managing projects of all sizes efficiently, and empowering people through skills transfer and collaboration.

Our Culture and Principles

DSM believes in fair business negotiation where all parties benefit equitably. We aim to build partnerships rooted in trust, professionalism, and long-term mutual success.
